The Idea

The idea for organicsDE originated in 2010, when Germany born Oliver Meister grazed the Viktualienmarkt on one of his visits to Munich and realized how much he missed the specialties of his home town. One local product in particular is impossible to find anywhere in the United States, a Pesto made from the Ur-Kraut Baerlauch (Wild Garlic). On a mission to bring Baerlauch and its unique flavor to the US, partnerships with local growers and manufactures were established. OrganicsDE is committed to only sourcing products and ingredients that are 100% natural, either grown wild or cultivated adhering to Organic and Biodynamic farming standards. Preserving cultural traditions, from the field to your table.
